Our Story

Founded in 2023 in the heart of Kampala, EveCodes Coffee was born from a simple passion — to create a warm, welcoming space where exceptional coffee brings people together.

We proudly source our beans from the fertile hills of Uganda, supporting local farmers who grow some of the world’s finest Arabica and Robusta coffee. Every cup tells a story of dedication, tradition, and community.

From our cozy corner café, we've grown into a place where students study, friends laugh, professionals work, and memories are made — one perfectly brewed cup at a time.
